⚔️ Game Design Intern — Blizzard Entertainment
Location: Irvine, CA | On-site Preferred
Program Duration: 12 weeks | Summer 2026
Reports to: Lead Designer, Franchise Team (e.g., Overwatch, Diablo, World of Warcraft)

About the Role
At Blizzard, gameplay comes first. As a Game Design Intern, you’ll help craft the systems, encounters, or balance that make our worlds legendary. You’ll learn Blizzard’s iterative design process — from first concept to final polish — while working alongside some of the most seasoned designers in the industry.

What You’ll Do
Collaborate on system, level, or encounter design for a Blizzard franchise project.

Author clear, concise design documentation and update it through iteration cycles.

Assist in balancing combat, items, or progression systems based on testing data.

Participate in internal playtests, capture feedback, and propose improvements to enhance player experience.

Work with cross-functional teams (engineering, art, audio, QA) to bring features to completion.

Present end-of-term project outcomes and learnings to the design department.

What We’re Looking For
Enrolled in a degree program in Game Design, Computer Science, Interactive Media, or related field, graduating May 2026 – July 2027.

A player-first mindset with the ability to analyze what makes games fun, fair, and meaningful.

Strong written and verbal communication — can clearly explain design reasoning.

Experience with scripting or modding tools (e.g., Lua, Blueprints, Unity, StarCraft/Warcraft editors).

Comfortable iterating on feedback and polishing until “it feels right.”

Bonus Skills
Familiarity with one or more Blizzard franchises.

Knowledge of game balance, economy, or progression systems.

Basic experience reading player metrics or telemetry data.

Why Blizzard?

You’ll step into a studio that values craftsmanship, teamwork, and passion for play. Your internship will give you hands-on experience in AAA systems and insight into how designers at Blizzard iterate toward their trademark “it just feels right” gameplay.
